Australia's Changing Climate
CSIRO Climate Science Centre · NSW Stage 5 Science
SC5-12ES SC5-13ES
Lesson Overview
Year 9 60 min Science
Students will investigate evidence for climate change using real CSIRO and BOM data, explain the impact on Australian ecosystems, and evaluate human responses at local and national levels.
Activities
🗺 NSW local context — 2019–20 Black Summer Bushfires
The Black Summer fires burned 5.5 million hectares across NSW. CSIRO analysis links the severity directly to climate-change-driven warming and drought — a powerful real-world hook for Stage 5 Earth Systems outcomes.
